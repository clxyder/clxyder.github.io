Research

I work in two labs the Power Systems Group with Dr. Mischa Steurer and in the Decision & Secure Systems lab with Dr. Moses Anubi. The unique position I am in allows me to grasp the intricacies of controls and numerical optimzation and apply it to the world of power systems.

Power Systems Group

I am working on a reconfiguration algorithm for shipboard power systems, expanding the algorithm's capabilities and updating the shipboard power system models on digital real-time simulator RTDS.

Decision & Secure Systems

Currently working on distributed fault tolerant controllers for space applications.
